Use Cases

Creative Reference / Inspiration

  • Professional creative and hobbyists use Piccsy for reference, inspiration, and portfolio promotion.

Social Upkeep

  • Tracking images of your friends and family across social networks has become increasingly difficult.
  • Your friends are participating in communities including Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus and Instagram, as well as many more you've never even heard of.
  • These sites have not been optimized for image discovery, creating platforms that are difficult to navigate through.
  • Piccsy will solve this problem by integrating APIs into our streams, allowing you to track our friends activity using an appealing interface.

Stock Photography

  • Our advisor, Bruce Livingstone, pioneered Microstock photography by sourcing images from amateur photographers as well as professionals.
  • We envision Piccsy taking this a step further, enabled by curation, to archive a larger image repository than any other stock site on the Internet.

Image Search

  • Google Images gets the job done, but fails to deliver an inviting user experience.
  • By integrating the social graph, image recognition technology and an immersive user experience, we are confident that Piccsy will be able to offer a more enjoyable image discovery platform.

Entertainment

  • Viewing celebrity photos, memes, and comics, are only a few of the ways people currently enjoy images online.
  • With our contextual, personalized streams, and great user experience, we make it easier to have fun looking at images on the internet.

Online Shopping

  • Piccsy is widely used to view images of art, furniture, fashion and other retail products.
  • In-image advertising will be a promising opportunity, leveraging both our image discovery product and the Piccsy Places platform.

Adult Media

  • Piccsy's combination of customized streams and filters allows our audience to easily see any image-based content they're interested in, including adult media.
  • We currently require an opt-in, ensuring users won't come across NSFW images anywhere on the site unless they've chosen to.

Portfolio

  • The online portfolio space is due for a much-needed facelift, and we have some great ideas of how Piccsy can be fashioned to accomplish this task.

Image Hosting

  • There are currently many popular image hosting solutions, but we anticipate with growth, product evolution, network effects and partnerships/integrations, Piccsy will emerge as an industry leader.

Product

Piccsy Users:

  • Discover images through 'streams' that filter content based on custom parameters set by our community.
  • Catalog images into galleries based on theme, interest, or any other orientation.
  • Create a unique image representation/repository that they can then share within the Piccsy community, and throughout the Internet.

Market/Competition

Photo-sharing is one of the most popular activities on the Internet today. Facebook alone has over 200 million photos uploaded daily.

Piccsy recognizes the social/cataloguing void Google Images suffers from, as well as Tumblr's fragmented, unsearchable network, and Pinterest's reliance on user only bookmarking.

By providing personalized streams using a uniform display, Piccsy is a social platform that aggregates images from around the web, based on customized parameters set by the user, offering a social way to discover images.
